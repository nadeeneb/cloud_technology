Minecraft

Minecraft is pretty much about what the name says. The premise is to mine, and to craft. The goal of the game is to defeat the Ender Dragon, but there are quite a few steps to be able to access the dimension in which it resides, such as gathering tools and materials to open up the portal. However, this game is up to the player's imagination in what they choose to do.
